DropBox

The Purpose of this project

The goal is to create a platform where we can keep our photos safe.

Target Audience

People who want to keep their photos safe and don't want to store them in local storage.

Justifications

When photos are in local storage, there is a possibility of losing photos as a result of any damage to the device.

Benefits of the Projects

  • Our photos are safe
  • If the device is damaged, our photos will not be lost.
  • Can access photos from anywhere in the world.

Usage

Since the project is a web application, you can use it on all platforms.
